# OctoAcme Organizational Activities

## Purpose
Support employee engagement, learning, and community building through organized activities and clubs.

## Overview
OctoAcme offers a variety of employee-led clubs and activities to foster collaboration, personal development, and work-life balance. These activities provide opportunities for team members to connect across departments, explore shared interests, and build relationships outside of project work.

## Available Activities

### Manga Maniacs

**Schedule:** Tuesdays at 7pm

**Max Attendance:** 15 people

**How to Join:** Contact the club organizer through the internal activities portal or sign up during the next session.

## Starting a New Activity

Interested in starting a new club or activity? Follow these steps:

1. Create a proposal with:
   - Activity name and description
   - Proposed schedule
   - Expected attendance
   - Required resources (meeting space, materials, etc.)

2. Submit your proposal to the Employee Engagement team

3. Once approved, promote your activity through internal channels

4. Maintain regular communication with participants and update schedules as needed

## Activity Guidelines

- **Inclusivity:** All activities should be welcoming to team members of all skill levels and backgrounds
- **Scheduling:** Activities should be scheduled to minimize conflicts with core working hours when possible
- **Space:** Coordinate with facilities to reserve appropriate meeting spaces
- **Communication:** Keep participants informed of schedule changes and important updates
- **Feedback:** Collect participant feedback regularly to improve the experience
